East-West Armwrestling 2024

Date: September 20 - October 2, 2024
Location: Astana, Barys Arena

Over two weeks, competitors will vie for supremacy in various weight classes, showcasing their strength and skill. This highly anticipated event promises intense matches and an exciting atmosphere. Armwrestling Asian Championship

Date: November 5 - November 10, 2024
Location: Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan
The Armwrestling Asian Championship 2024 will showcase the finest arm wrestlers from across Asia in an exhilarating competition.
