Levan Saginashvili

Levan Saginashvili, nicknamed the Georgian Hulk, is a Georgian professional arm-wrestler who holds the World's number 1 ranking on the Right Hand. Winner of the World Armwrestling Championship seven times, the European arm-wrestling championship six times, the Vendetta Top 8 four times, and the current holder of the East vs West Super Heavyweight Championship, Saginashvili is widely regarded as the strongest arm-wrestler in history.

Denis Cyplenkov

Denis Cyplenkov is a renowned Ukrainian arm wrestler and strongman, known for his incredible strength and massive physique. Standing at 6'1" (186 cm) and weighing around 290 lbs (130 kg), he has won multiple international arm wrestling championships. His massive hands and forearms are legendary in the sport, contributing to his dominance in competitions. Apart from arm wrestling, Cyplenkov has also participated in strongman events, further showcasing his extraordinary physical abilities. He is regarded as one of the strongest arm wrestlers in history.

Alizhan Muratov

Alizhan Muratov is a highly skilled arm wrestling champion from Kazakhstan, known for his rapid rise in the sport. He has earned multiple titles in regional and international competitions, showcasing both strength and exceptional technique. Muratov's disciplined training and strategic approach to matches have made him a formidable competitor, often defeating more experienced opponents. His consistent performance in championships has earned him a reputation as one of the top emerging arm wrestlers, and he continues to gain recognition for his achievements on the world stage.
